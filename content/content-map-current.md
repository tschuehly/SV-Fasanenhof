## Content Overlaps and Issues

### Overlapping Content
1. **Club information**: Both sites have basic club info
2. **Contact details**: Same address and phone on both
3. **Board/Leadership**: BSG references main club
4. **History**: BSG references main club
5. **Statutes**: BSG mentions but references main club

### Issues
1. **Fragmented information**: Main site has almost no detail about archery
2. **Inconsistent navigation**: Users must navigate between two different sites
3. **URL confusion**: Two different domains for the same organization
4. **Maintenance burden**: Two separate CMS platforms (WordPress + Jimdo)
5. **SEO impact**: Split authority between two domains
6. **User experience**: Confusing for visitors to understand relationship
7. **Content duplication**: Basic club info appears on both sites
